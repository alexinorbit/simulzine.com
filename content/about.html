Hi there !

I' m Alex. SIMULZINE is mostly my blog.

This is about what i'm learning partly as a programmer, partly as a designer of interactive applications.

Six years ago I co-founded orbitlab, a creative studio focused on interactive media. Since then I have been involved in all kinds of projects with big companies, research centers and other organizations.

I like to experiment and favor state of the art practices for my projects. I am a great fan of horizontal collaborative teams that have clear aims. The potential of the internet as a co-working space has already revealed itself and it's up to adventurous initiatives that will take advantage of the web's unique abilities to narrow distances and bring people from around the world together to work on common software projects. Free/open source software or Wikipedia, are indications of larger possibilities.
